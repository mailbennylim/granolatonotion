1. Integration token — go to notion.so/profile/integrations → New integration → copy the secret. Then in your Granola database in Notion, click ... → Connections → add your integration.

2. Database ID — open your Granola database. The URL looks like notion.so/yourname/abc123...?v=... — copy the 32 characters between your name and the ?.
